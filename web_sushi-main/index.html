Our Speciality

Kani Sushi

Kani sushi is a bit of a blanket term used in sushi restaurants for crab sushi dishes.It comprises steamed rice and crab meat wrapped in seaweed, usually served with soy sauce and wasabi on the side! Sushi contains various nutrients such as protein, omega-three fatty acids, and vitamins A and B12.

California Maki

Uramakizushi containing cucumber, crab or imitation crab, and avocado,It contains 255 calories. The 7 grams of fat in this much California roll is mostly from the avocado, which is mostly healthy, monounsaturated fat. You'll also get 9 grams of protein from a California roll made with imitation crab meat, which is actually fish.

Salmon Sashimi

Salmon sashimi is a delicacy made from thinly sliced raw fish served with sauce,condiments, and garnishes. When prepared right, salmon sashimi tastes exceptional and has numerous health benefits. However,salmon used for this dish must be entirely safe for raw consumption. Sashimi is safe to eat without heat treatment.

Tuna Sashimi

Tuna sashimi is a delicacy made from thinly sliced tuna combined with different condiments and garnishes. It can be made from different parts of tuna, but it’s known as one of the most popular sashimi types. It’s rich in omega-3 fatty acids, iron, B vitamins, potassium, and selenium, which can all be highly beneficial.Sashimi usually implies other fresh and healthy vegetables.

Tonkotsu Ramen

Tonkotsu ramen is a deliciously creamy pork bone broth traditionally served up with long thin noodles and topped with a variety of ingredients.This broth is also rich in minerals as the marrow and fats from the bone release all the good stuff too. The soup has a silky texture and a cloudy coloring – Tonkotsu is truly heartwarming stuff,drinking also the soup of ramen makes you satisfied.

Seafood Ramen

The Seafood Ramen Noodle Soup is traditionally made withseafood.In Today's dish has a base of our homemade fish stock made with seafood as the main protein.In Seafood Ramen Noodle We added tamari sauce and sesame oil used for sauteing the seafood. It is a simple soup to make and we used fresh angel hair noodles as a substitute for the ramen noodles.
